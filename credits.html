Wightwatch Keep: Credits

Credit where credit is due...

Software

Wightwatch Keep was made with open-source software including: the Godot Engine, Pixeloma for creating and modifying the partwork, and Audacity for modifying sound and music.

Artwork

Assets from 1 bit dungeon tileset by RGS_Dev and 1-Bit Patterns and Tiles by PiiiXL were used alongside original work.

Sounds / Music

Sounds were sourced from 1-bit Dungeon Brawler Asset Pack by StevePixelFace and various public domain effects. Music based on original work by HydroGene (MonsterVania 8-bit Pack #1 and High Quality 8-bit / Chiptune Musics

Thanks

Thanks to my sons for playtesting, to my wife for supporting me while I built this in my free time, and to you for playing!
